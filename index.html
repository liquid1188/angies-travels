Tasty treats · No. 03

Meals worth flying for.

The dishes that ruined every other version of themselves. Where to eat them, and how much they actually cost.

Bologna · Pasta

A bowl of tagliatelle al ragù at Trattoria di Via Serra.

Hand-cut, slow-simmered, and €14. Walk fifteen minutes from the station, sit at the counter, order it without thinking.
Mexico City · Street

The al pastor at El Vilsito, after midnight.

A mechanic's garage by day, a taquería by night. The pineapple is on the trompo. The salsa verde is the right answer.
